How do I access my purchased digital books?

Upon purchasing a digital book from our store, you can easily access it through two ways. Firstly, we will send the purchased e-book and audio book to your registered email account within 24 hours of your order. Secondly, we will also share a secure drive link that contains the e-book and audio book for easy access. You can simply click on the drive link and access your purchased books from any device, anywhere and anytime. Rest assured, our team ensures that your digital books are delivered to you promptly and securely.

Can I gift a digital book to someone else?

Absolutely! At Uncharted Pages, we offer the option to purchase digital books as a gift for someone special. This can be a perfect way to share the joy of reading with a friend, family member, or loved one. When you purchase a digital book as a gift, you will have the opportunity to enter the recipient's email address during checkout. Once the purchase is complete, the book will be delivered directly to their email inbox, allowing them to enjoy it at their leisure.
